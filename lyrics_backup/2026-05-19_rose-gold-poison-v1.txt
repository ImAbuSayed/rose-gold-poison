[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook 1]
Kaali raat de hanere vich baldi ae fire
Main takda haan tainu mera dilda desire
Teri zulfan da jaadu mainu karda ae slow
Main labhda haan tainu you already know

[Hook 2]
Hawaavan de vich ghul gayi teri fragrance
Main jhumda haan kalla doing my dance
Bulliyan cho huss ke tu kardi ae glow
Main rukda nahi hunn keeping it slow

[Verse 1]
Kaale hanere vich main turda haan fast
Manzil ae sadhi door getting so vast
Tere piche main ghumda haan ban ke shadow
Teri nakhre di agg vich disda ae glow
Pehli takni ch main bhul gaya my pain
Mere dil di zameen te pawein tu rain
Raat di chuppi ch main sunda haan tere saah
Tainu milan layi labhda haan right jeha raah

[Pre-Chorus 1]
Hawa vich ud-da ae dhuaan jado main saah lainda haan
Kalla beh ke main yaadan vich going so deep
Sard raatan ch main karda haan miss teri warmth
Mere jazbaat vi check karde my strength

[Chorus 1]
Akhiyan ch paa ke akhiyan you take my soul
Main rukda nahi kade you making me whole
Mera dil dhadkarda ae tezz in the night
Tainu takk ke hundi ae magic jehi light
Nasha ban ke raggan vich flowing so tezz
Main chahunda haan tainu winning this race
Tere utte marda haan giving my all
Main ud-da haan hawa vich waiting for your call

[Verse 2]
Kach de glass vich shadow disdi ae dark
Main turda haan kalla leaving my mark
Leather di jacket vich main lukaunda haan pain
Teri wafa di udeek vich standing in rain
Shehar di sarkan te main labhda haan truth
Zehar jehi nazran naal you stealing my youth
Kalla lad-da haan duniya naal like a king
Main jittna ae tainu doing my thing

[Pre-Chorus 2]

[Chorus 2]
Akhiyan ch paa ke akhiyan you take my soul
Main rukda nahi kade you making me whole
Mera dil dhadkarda ae tezz in the night
Tainu takk ke hundi ae magic jehi light
Nasha ban ke raggan vich flowing so tezz
Main chahunda haan tainu winning this race
Tere utte marda haan giving my all
Main ud-da haan hawa vich waiting for your call

[Verse 3]
Teri khamoshi di aawaz is making me numb
Main turda haan kalla feeling so dumb
Kandhan naal gallan main karda haan roz
Baraf jeha hunda main freezing my toes
Khushiyan di thaan utte looking for light
Mere raste ch kaante ne fighting this fight
Main haarda haan duniya just to be yours
Main khol da haan baby mere dil de doors

[Bridge]
Chup jehi raat ch main haan kalla khada
Saeed ban gaya ae tera deewana bada
Main ronda haan andar ton holding back tear
Mere dil di pukaar mainu kardi ae clear
Ishq di ae bedi sailing too far
Main labhda haan manzil seeking your pyaar

[Final Chorus]
Akhiyan ch paa ke akhiyan you take my soul
Main rukda nahi kade you making me whole
Mera dil dhadkarda ae tezz in the night
Tainu takk ke hundi ae magic jehi light
Nasha ban ke raggan vich flowing so tezz
